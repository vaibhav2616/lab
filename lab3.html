About the Artist

About Atif Aslam Atif Aslam is a renowned Pakistani singer, songwriter, and actor, celebrated for his melodious voice and captivating stage presence. He is one of the most successful and beloved artists in South Asia, known for his versatility and ability to deliver emotions through music.

Music Career
Debut:
Atif started his music journey as the lead vocalist of the band Jal with the hit song Aadat (2003). After parting ways with the band, he pursued a solo career, releasing his debut album Jal Pari in 2004.
